RSVP Speed Reader

What is RSVP?
Rapid Serial Visual Presentation is a method of reading where words are displayed one by one at a fixed focal point. This eliminates saccades (eye movements) and reduces subvocalization, allowing you to read significantly faster than traditional methods.

How to Use

1. Upload a PDF using the button on the right.
2. Adjust Speed using the "WPM" (Words Per Minute) slider in the top-right overlay.
3. Press Play to begin reading.

* Privacy Note: All processing happens locally in your browser via WebAssembly. Your files are never uploaded to a server.
